OUR PROJECTS

INITIATIVES THAT MAKE A DIFFERENCE

Discover the impactful projects led by our Prefects' Guild, fostering community, leadership, and excellence.

Annual Sports Meet

Our flagship event brings together students from all grades in exciting inter-house competitions. From track and field to team sports, we promote physical health, sportsmanship, and school spirit.

  • Inter-house athletics and team games
  • Leadership roles in event organization
  • Community participation and cheering
  • Awards ceremony recognizing excellence
🏆 Athletics 🤝 Teamwork

Community Clean-up

Environmental stewardship is at our core. We organize regular clean-up drives in the school premises and surrounding areas, teaching students the importance of sustainability and community responsibility.

  • Monthly school and neighborhood clean-ups
  • Tree planting and garden maintenance
  • Educational sessions on environmental care
  • Partnerships with local environmental groups
🌿 Environment 🌍 Community

Leadership Workshops

Through interactive workshops, we equip students with essential leadership skills, communication techniques, and personal development strategies to become confident and responsible leaders.

  • Public speaking and presentation skills
  • Conflict resolution and team building
  • Time management and goal setting
  • Mentorship programs for junior students
🎯 Leadership 💪 Development

Cultural Festivals

Celebrating diversity through cultural events, traditional performances, and art exhibitions that showcase our rich heritage.

🎨 Culture 🎭 Arts

Student Mentorship

Pairing senior students with juniors for academic and personal guidance, fostering a supportive learning environment.

📚 Education 🤝 Support
