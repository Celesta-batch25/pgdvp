ABOUT THE GUILD

The Prefects' Guild of Dharmapala Vidyalaya Pannipitiya is the voice of student leadership, service, and discipline. We support the school community by encouraging respectful conduct, organizing student initiatives, and promoting excellence across academics, sports, and civic life.

OUR MISSION

Student Leadership

Empower peers through example, guidance, and a strong sense of responsibility.

Community Service

Build a supportive and inclusive school environment for all learners.

Academic Integrity

Promote fairness, honest effort, and excellence in every school activity.
