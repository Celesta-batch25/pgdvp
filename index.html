OUR MISSION

The Prefects' Guild of Dharmapala Vidyalaya Pannipitiya is the pinnacle of student leadership. Our mission is to maintain the highest standards of discipline while fostering a culture of integrity, service, and excellence. We act as the vital link between the school administration and the student body.
